Frequently Asked Questions

What age is this course suitable for?

The course is designed for students around 12 years old and above, though motivated younger students are also welcome. We adapt the pace to each student's level.

Do students need prior programming experience?

No prior experience is needed! We start from the basics and gradually build up to more advanced topics. Enthusiasm and willingness to learn are all that's required.

What equipment do students need?

Students need a computer (Windows, Mac, or Linux) and a stable internet connection for online classes. All software we use is free and we'll help with installation.

How long is the complete course?

The complete course covers 12 comprehensive modules plus AI projects. Duration varies based on class schedule and student pace, typically 6-12 months for the full curriculum.

Are classes available in English and Vietnamese?

Yes! All course materials are bilingual, and we can conduct classes in either English or Vietnamese based on student preference and comfort level.

What makes this course different?

We combine real-world industry experience with age-appropriate teaching methods. Students learn through practical projects, including exciting AI applications, while building strong fundamentals in programming and algorithms.
